# Research Agenda — 2026-03-24

## Context

Our product: **Equalize Digital Accessibility Checker** (WP plugin, 10K active installs)
- Scans WordPress posts/pages for WCAG, ADA, Section 508 issues
- Free tier + paid plans ($190–$2,250/year)
- Unlimited scans per site (key differentiator)

Competitors in focus: AAArdvark, Ally, WPAccessibility

## Competitive Snapshot (Lead Research — Cycle 1)

### AAArdvark
- **Model**: SaaS + WP plugin (external platform, API key required)
- **Pricing**: $49–$499/month ($549–$5,499/year) based on site/page count
- **Installs**: Low (plugin is a display layer; primary product is cloud)
- **Differentiators**: Visual mode (see issues overlaid on live site), unlimited users/teams, remediation tracking
- **Weakness**: Page limits on all paid plans; expensive for high-volume sites
- Source: https://aaardvarkaccessibility.com/pricing/

### Ally (by Elementor)
- **Model**: Freemium WP plugin + SaaS AI fixes
- **Pricing**: Free (5 URL scans) → ~$5+/month (25 scans, 2,500 AI fixes/year)
- **Installs**: 300K+ (inherits from "One Click Accessibility" / Elementor ecosystem)
- **Differentiators**: AI-powered fixes, Elementor distribution (12M+ users), usability widget
- **Weakness**: Scan credits are limited; AI fixes burn a separate credit pool
- Source: https://elementor.com/help/ally-plans/

### WP Accessibility
- **Model**: Free, no paid tier
- **Pricing**: $0 forever
- **Installs**: 60K+ active, 1.75M downloads
- **Differentiators**: Theme-level fixes (skip links, focus outlines, alt enforcement), color contrast checker — works without scanning
- **Weakness**: Not a scanner — detects no post-level content issues; no remediation workflow
- Source: https://wordpress.org/plugins/wp-accessibility/

## Open Questions

Ranked by business impact:

1. **How does Ally get distributed — is it bundled with Elementor installs or opt-in?** If bundled, that's a structural moat we can't fight with marketing alone.
2. **What do WP Accessibility users say they need that the plugin doesn't provide?** Forum/review mining could reveal direct upgrade intent.
3. **Does AAArdvark target agencies specifically?** Their unlimited-users model suggests agency focus — if so, do we compete there or cede it?
4. **What is the European Accessibility Act (EAA) deadline effect?** EAA enforcement began June 2025 — are new buyers entering the market who don't know any of these tools yet?
