## Findings Summary

### AAArdvark
- SaaS + WP plugin. Pricing: $49–$499/month, page-limited (100–10,000 pages).
- All features on all plans; unlimited users. Visual overlay mode is standout feature.
- Weakness: page limits create cost scaling problems for large/complex sites.
- Source: https://aaardvarkaccessibility.com/pricing/

### Ally (Elementor)
- Free + paid (~$5+/month). Backed by Elementor (12M+ users).
- 300K+ active installs (plugin formerly "One Click Accessibility").
- AI-powered fixes with credit model. Usability widget (overlay) included free.
- Weakness: limited scan credits; AI fix credits are finite and tied to plan tier.
- Source: https://elementor.com/help/ally-plans/ | https://wordpress.org/plugins/pojo-accessibility/

### WP Accessibility
- Free only. 60K+ active installs. Author: Joe Dolson.
- Theme-level fixes — NOT a scanner. No post-level content issue detection.
- Color contrast checker, diagnostic CSS. Completely different product category.
- Source: https://wordpress.org/plugins/wp-accessibility/

### Our Position (Equalize Digital)
- 10K active installs (vs Ally's 300K, WP Accessibility's 60K).
- Unlimited scans per site — structural advantage vs AAArdvark's page limits.
- $190/year for 1 site — cheaper than AAArdvark One Site ($549/year).
- Source: https://equalizedigital.com/accessibility-checker/pricing/
